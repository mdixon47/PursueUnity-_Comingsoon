The Pursue Unity's Walk

Supporting emerging urban leaders in pursuit of higher education.

Pursue Scholars’ staff member, Solomon Dixon, will walk the entirety of Western Avenue from south to north in an effort to help unify Chicago. Western is the longest continuous street in the city of Chicago, totaling around 24 miles. As his journey covers the expanse of Chicago and traverses through many of its unique and vibrant neighborhoods, Solomon’s path will both physically and psychologically represent the city.
